# Product Roadmap

## Wave 1: Foundation

- Identity, onboarding, profile, and baseline planner
- Syllabus graph and chapter progression tracking
- Basic test engine and analytics

## Wave 2: Intelligence

- Adaptive schedule balancing and backlog recovery
- Weakness diagnostics and action recommendations
- Mentor workflows and moderated discussion streams

## Wave 3: National scale

- Multilingual delivery and low-bandwidth enhancements
- NGO and school deployment kits
- Open data standards for curriculum portability
