# Launch Strategy: Largest Open JEE Platform

## 100-city rollout model
- City ambassadors and mentor volunteers
- School and coaching integration drives
- Monthly public leaderboard for community contribution, not student ranking

## Community growth loops
- Open issue sprints for feature delivery
- Educator-authored public curriculum packs
- Translation hackathons

## Adoption milestones
- 10k active learners in pilot wave
- 100k active learners in year one
- 500k active learners in year two
