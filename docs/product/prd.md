# Product Requirements Document

## Problem Statement
Class 11 entrants preparing for JEE face fragmented systems for school, coaching, and self-study. This causes poor consistency, unmanaged backlog, and avoidable burnout.

## Goals
- Build a free, open, scalable prep operating system.
- Improve consistency and planning reliability.
- Reduce backlog and increase concept mastery.
- Keep accessibility and low-bandwidth support first class.

## Personas
- Student (primary)
- Parent/Guardian (limited visibility)
- Mentor/Teacher
- Moderator/Admin

## Core Capability Areas
- Planner and schedule reconciliation
- Learning graph and concept progression
- Practice, tests, and adaptive diagnostics
- Doubt resolution and structured explanations
- Wellness and sustainable prep monitoring

## Non-Goals (v1)
- Paid coaching marketplace
- Ad-based monetization
- Closed-source recommendation black box
