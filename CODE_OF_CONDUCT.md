# Code of Conduct

UdaanJEE OS is committed to a safe, inclusive, and respectful community.

## Expectations

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on ideas, not individuals.
- Support learners from diverse backgrounds.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, bullying
- Doxxing or privacy violations
- Academic shaming

## Reporting

Report concerns to maintainers via documented governance channels.
